1. What is Bootstrap? 
  It is free and open-source, and it is directed at responsive, mobile-first front-end web development 
  It contains HTML, CSS, and JavaScript-based design templates for typography, forms, buttons, navigation, and other interface components 

when to use bootstrap: designing a mobile first approach and getting uniform design
when not to use bootstrap: simple websites or complex,fully customized websites
